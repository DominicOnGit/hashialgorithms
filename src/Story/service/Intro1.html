Sometimes the solution for a part of the puzzle is easy to see. E.g. if there is an island that needs 4 bridges, and that island has only two neighbouring islands, then we need to connect both neighbours with double bridges.
Let's try to formulate this rule as an algorithm:
  1. Find a vertex
    • with targetDegree 4
    • and two incident edges
  2. then select one of the incident edges
    • that has multiplicity less than 2
  3. then increment the multiplicty of that edge.
